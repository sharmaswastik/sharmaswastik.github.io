About Me

I graduated in 2021 with a B.Tech degree from the National Institute of Technology Srinagar in Electrical Engineering. After that, I joined the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur in July 2021 to pursue a PhD. My research interests include Transactive Energy Modelling, EV Charging Infrastructure, Electricity Market Design, Deep Reinforcement Learning applications to Power Systems, and Integrated Transmission and Distribution Modelling.
